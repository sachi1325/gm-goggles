PatternsiDetected PatternsAutomatic analysis of your glucose data against clinical guidelines. Checks time-in-range goal (≥70%), hypoglycemia frequency (<4% below 70), hyperglycemia (<25% above 140), glucose variability (CV ≤36%), and dawn phenomenon (early-morning rise).
Full Glucose TraceiFull Glucose TraceYour complete glucose history across the selected time window. Resolution buttons (5m, 15m, 1h…) control how readings are averaged into each point — useful for smoothing noisy data over longer periods. Hover any point to see the exact value and time.

Daily Time in RangeiDaily Time in RangeEach point shows what percentage of that day's readings fell into each zone. The green line (In range) is the main trend — aim for it to stay above the dashed 70% goal line. Amber (High) and red (Low) lines show hyperglycemia and hypoglycemia burden. Use the toggle buttons to show/hide individual lines.

Hourly Average GlucoseiHourly Average GlucoseA heatmap showing your average glucose for each hour of the day (midnight to 11 PM), averaged across the selected time range. Green = in range, amber = high, red = low. Darker/brighter cells indicate readings further from the centre of your target range. Hover a cell to see the exact average.
Each cell = average glucose for that hour across the selected period. Hover to see value.
Hourly Trend ChartiHourly Trend ChartA bar chart of your average glucose for each hour, colour-coded by zone. This reveals recurring daily patterns — post-meal spikes, overnight lows, dawn phenomenon (rising glucose in the early morning hours). Filtered by the same time range as the heatmap above.
